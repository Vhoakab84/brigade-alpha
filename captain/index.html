  • Start by getting familiar with our Captains' program: Slide Deck
  • Join the Brigade Organizers group & introduce yourself on the Introductions thread.

Are you ready to captain a brigade?

We need leaders who will stand up, be counted, and work together.

The Captains' program has annual classes that start in August. Applications to the program are open from March 1 - May 31 each year.

What we ask

  1. Form a partnership with your city based on our core activities
  2. Host a recurring Brigade Meetup, at least monthly
  3. Participate in Brigade events & campaigns a couple of times a year
  4. Participate in hangouts and facilitated sessions with other captains
  5. Share the stories of your local activities with the Brigade community
  6. Recruit new members to CfA Brigade
  7. One year commitment

What we offer

  • Small budget for hosting meetups, events and organizing campaigns
  • Invitation to CfA Summit
  • Training from the Code for America Academy
  • Connections to Code for America networks
  • Hosted & facilitated networking with other Brigade members
  • Features in Code for America publications
  • Free cloud resources for your Brigade's projects
  • Free books for your Brigade's library
  • @codeforamerica.org account
  • Code for America track jacket & gear
