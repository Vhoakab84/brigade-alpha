Help your local government get open:

  • Speak Before Council

    Every city council provides open time for citizens to speak. Prepare a statement and let your council know about Code for America. Contact us to find out more!

  • Inform Strategy, Policy & Resolutions

    Innovative governments welcome civic-minded citizens to engage in the process of shaping the digital cities and communities in which they live. Contact us to find out more!

  • Start or Join a City Volunteer Program

    Some cities are opening new channels for citizens to participate in recognized volunteer programs for civic tech. Contact us find out more!

  • Anchor an Open Data Portal

    More local governments each day are investing in technology infrastructure for open data but they need help from people who understand how data are used.Contact us to find out more!

  • Help Gov Go Open Source

    More local governments each day are moving to open source software but they need help from people who already understand the dynamics of open source to get them where they need to be. Contact us to find out more!
